Training

How I've prepared to do it

  • Age 6

    Why, yes, I do know that song!

    One of my earliest memories is when my aunt visited us from California. She asked me if I knew the song "Somewhere Over the Rainbow". I nodded and started singing. Unfortunately for everyone, I didn't know how to end the song, so I just kept repeating the first verse. She eventually cut me off.

    Since then, I've taken voice lessons from Kathy Ludowise and Daniel Hughes.

  • College

    Could you please stop tapping?

    My freshman year at Purdue, a friend invited me to drop in on the tap class that he was taking. Fast forward to my senior year. One Sunday morning, I was enthusiastically practicing on the vinyl kitchen floor in my second floor apartment. After a few minutes, I heard a knock on the door. My downstairs neighbor, nursing a hangover, calmly asked me to stop tap dancing, because our kitchen was right over her bedroom.

    Since then, I've taken tap lessons from Dotty Lester-White, Fosse dance from Donna Cerio, and Jazz at Marie Stinnett's studio.

  • Summer 2003

    Say that with a Southern accent!

    I took advantage of a layoff notice to sign up for a nine-week Theatre Artists Institute workshop one summer. Forty hours per week included dialect training from Kimberly Mohne Hill and acting training from Greg Fritsch.

My Fair Lady

In the brand new Lohman Theatre

November 2008 at Foothill College Lohman Theatre

In this Foothill Musical Theatre production, I played Alfie's friend Jamie, and a servant in Professor's home, and a dancer at the Ascot. Watching the amazing Kit Wilder prepare for each performance, when he practiced synchronizing words of his monologue with putting sugar cubes into his tea cup, was a great education in the craft of acting.

Pippin

January 2007 at Foothill College Lohman Theatre

In this Foothill Musical Theatre production, I played a soldier, the decapitated Talking Head of a different soldier, and a dancer in the ensemble. To feel comfortable taking off my shirt on stage, I lost 10 pounds and did situps before each performance.
